Body Mass Index (BMI) is a person's weight in kilograms divided by the square of their height in meters. It provides a rough guide to whether your weight is within the normal range.

BMI Categories:

  • Underweight: BMI < 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5–24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25–29.9
  • Obesity: BMI 30 or greater
