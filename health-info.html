Nutrition

Eat a balanced diet with plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid processed foods and sugary drinks.

Exercise

Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise most days of the week. This can include walking, cycling, or any activity that gets your heart rate up.

Common Health Issues

Stay informed about common health issues and how to prevent them. Regular check-ups with your doctor can help catch problems early.
